$$ \text{Area} = \frac{x_0(y_1 - y_2) + x_1(y_2 - y_0) + x_2(y_0 - y_1)}{2} \cdot \left( n(1+n^2) \text{arccot}(n) - n^2 \right) $$

L(n, h) = 2*sqrt(1+n^2) * E(arctan(1/n), sqrt(1 - n^2h^2))
